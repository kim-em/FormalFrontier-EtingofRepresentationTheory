## Current frontier

Analysis of why it's hard: p ∈ P_λ and q ∈ Q_λ respect CANONICAL rows/columns (not T₁-relative), so the existing `column_perm_dominance` (for LEFT multiplication) doesn't directly apply. The proof must use the SYT structure of both T₁ and T₂ simultaneously.
