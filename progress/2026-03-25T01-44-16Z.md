identity showing `card(Σ h : ElemBicol, FiberPerm h) = n! × card(NNMatrixWithMargins)`.

Two recommended approaches documented in the docstring:
1. **Group action**: Define `MulAction` of `Perm(Fin n)` on `ElemBicol` by precomposition with

   orbits ≃ `NNMatrixWithMargins` (via fiber-size classification).
2. **Direct equiv**: Construct `(Σ h, FiberPerm h) ≃ Perm(Fin n) × NNMat` using cumulative
   block assignment and within-fiber sorted ordering.
