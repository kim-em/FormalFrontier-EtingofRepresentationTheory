## Technical notes

- `Representation.directSum` uses `DirectSum.lmap`, which is set up for
  `⨁ i, V i` with `Fintype ι` + `DecidableEq ι`. `Fin n` provides both.
- `TensorProduct.piScalarRight` in `LinearAlgebra/TensorProduct/Pi.lean` is
  the LinearEquiv form (there's also an `AlgEquiv` form in RingTheory).
- Used `DFinsupp.ext` to reduce direct-sum equality to component equality;
  `DirectSum.linearEquivFunOnFintype.symm` is treated as an injective
  coercion from `Fin n → L` to `⨁ _ : Fin n, L`.
- `LinearEquiv.finrank_map_eq` gives submodule pushforward-preserves-finrank
  directly — simpler than manually constructing `Submodule.equivMapOfInjective`
  whose semilinear output triggers typeclass issues with `finrank_eq`.
