### Proof attempts for FRT theorem (3 attempts, all blocked)

**Attempt 1: Branching rule approach**
Requires: corner cell definition, partition removal operation, branching bijection SYT(λ) ≅ ⊔_c SYT(λ\c), hook length product recurrence, and a telescoping product identity Σ_c ∏h(λ)/∏h(λ\c) = n. Each step needs ~50-100 lines of new definitions and proofs. Total: ~300-500 lines of new infrastructure.

**Attempt 2: Book's inductive approach (Vandermonde determinant)**
The book proves dim V_λ = n!/∏h via formula (5.17.1): dim V_λ = n!/∏l_j! × ∏(l_i - l_j). This combines both halves (representation theory + combinatorics) and cannot be used for the purely combinatorial statement `card_standardYoungTableau_eq` alone.

**Attempt 3: Hook walk / probabilistic approach**
Requires: random hook walk algorithm, probability computation that each SYT is produced with probability 1/∏h(i,j). Extremely involved to formalize.
