- Weight space decomposition of tensor power as direct sum
- Projector-trace identity: tr(PA) = tr(A|_{im(P)}) when [A,P] = 0
- PermTracePoly evaluation = trace of σ on V^{⊗n} at diagonal matrices
- Polynomial identity from Zariski density

### Analysis of Sorry 2 (`youngSym_charValue_orthogonality`)
Key finding: Sorry 2 is **equivalent** to `F_λ = α · S_λ` (the sum_youngSym_permTracePoly theorem). The existing proof structure in lines 706-835 proves F_λ = α · S_λ modulo Sorry 2, and Sorry 2 follows from F_λ = α · S_λ by coefficient extraction.

The orthogonality requires showing c_λ generates a minimal left ideal in ℚ[S_n]:
- `IsSemisimpleModule` is available from Maschke (with `NeZero (n! : ℚ)`)
- But semisimplicity alone gives that (1/α)c_λ is idempotent with complemented image
- Need: c_λ generates a **simple** (irreducible) module — this is the Specht module theory

Created sub-issue #1924 with detailed mathematical analysis.
