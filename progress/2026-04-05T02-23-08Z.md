## Current frontier

The `column_standard_in_span'` sorry in PolytabloidBasis.lean:847 remains open. Three possible approaches were identified:

1. **Restructure induction**: Eliminate the column-standard base case. For non-SYT σ, replace with right-coset representative via left absorption (`of(σ)*c_λ = of(σ*g)*c_λ`), then apply Garnir to the non-column-standard representative. Requires changing the well-founded induction structure.

2. **Direct algebraic proof**: Express `of(σ)*c_λ` as a linear combination of polytabloids using sandwich lemmas, idempotent properties, or other algebraic identities.

3. **Change polytabloid definition**: Use tabloid-specific column stabilizers (as in James' book) instead of the canonical one. This would make left-coset absorption work but requires major refactor.
