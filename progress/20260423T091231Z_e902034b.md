Indecomposability of Ẽ₇ (and T(1,2,5) by analogy) is bottlenecked by the
same fundamental technical difficulty as the Ẽ₆ analog:

**How to transfer the N-action from arm1 (vertex 1→0 via
(p,q) ↦ (p+q, p, q, Nq)) to leaf subspaces W₁(4) and W₂(4) that only
touch block A of V(0) through arms 2 and 3.**

All three cases (Ẽ₆ #2418, Ẽ₇ #2427, T(1,2,5) half of #2400) are
structurally the same obstruction. Solving any one of them via a
generalizable technique would likely unlock the others.
