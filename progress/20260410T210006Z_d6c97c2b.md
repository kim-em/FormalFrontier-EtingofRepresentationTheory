## Current frontier

4 sorry's remain in `exists_invariant_product_complement` (2 per symmetric case):

1. **`hcompl : IsCompl (V'₀.prod W'₀) C`** (lines 1709, 1780)
   - V'₀.prod W'₀ is the ℂ-span of X-orbit of pure element (v₁,0) or (0,w₁)
   - C = ker φ where φ = component_j₀ ∘ e ∘ of
   - Proof strategy: show φ|_{V'₀.prod W'₀} is isomorphism to N_j₀
     - Surjectivity: φ(v₁,0) generates N_j₀ (has order k_max, hj₀_ne + hk_j₀)
     - Dimension: finrank(V'₀.prod W'₀) = k_max (cyclic module dimension)
     - Both give disjointness + dim equality → IsCompl

   - Follows from ≥2 nontrivial PID summands (k_i ≥ 1)
   - ≥2 nontrivial summands because finrank(ker X) ≥ 2 and each contributes 1
