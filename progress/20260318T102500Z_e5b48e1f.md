### Blocker identified

All three sorry's are blocked by the same fundamental issue: the reflection functor definitions (`reflectionFunctorPlus`, `reflectionFunctorMinus`) use `Decidable.casesOn` on `DecidableEq Q v i` to define `obj` and `mapLinear` at each vertex. When composing F⁻ ∘ F⁺:

- Types don't reduce: `(reflectionFunctorPlus Q i hi ρ).obj j` remains as `Decidable.casesOn (inst j i) ...` and Lean cannot simplify without knowing the specific Decidable instance.
- Direct sum reindexing blocked: `ArrowsOutOf Q̄ᵢ i` and `ArrowsInto Q i` are propositionally but not definitionally equal (both reduce to `Σ j, (j ⟶ i)` after evaluating `if i = i`).
- The mathematical argument (first isomorphism theorem, `quotKerEquivOfSurjective`) is clear, but bridging the Lean types requires infrastructure that doesn't exist yet.

### Three approaches attempted

1. **Direct unfold + quotKerEquivOfSurjective**: Failed — type mismatch between `ArrowsOutOf Q̄ᵢ i`-indexed direct sum and `ArrowsInto Q i`-indexed direct sum.
2. **`convert` approach**: Failed — `convert` couldn't find a pattern to rewrite.
3. **`Submodule.liftQ` construction**: Blocked — defining the component maps requires the same Decidable.casesOn type transport.
