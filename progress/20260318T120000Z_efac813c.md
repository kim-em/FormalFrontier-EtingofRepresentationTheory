## Accomplished

Proved `Etingof.Problem6_9_1c` (issue #1010): when AB is nilpotent in a Q₂-representation, the operator (BA).prodMap(AB) on V × W is also nilpotent.

**Proof strategy:**
1. AB nilpotent ⟹ BA nilpotent via shift lemma: `(BA)^m ∘ B = B ∘ (AB)^m`, then `(BA)^(n+1) = B(AB)^nA = 0`.
2. `(f.prodMap g)^k = (f^k).prodMap (g^k)` by induction using `prodMap_mul`. Take `k = max(n, m)` to get both components zero.

**Key Mathlib APIs used:**
- `LinearMap.prodMap_mul`, `LinearMap.prodMap_one`, `LinearMap.prodMap_zero`
- `Module.End.mul_apply`, `pow_succ`, `pow_add`
