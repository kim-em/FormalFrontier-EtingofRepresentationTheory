  for a finite family of `Representation k (GL_N k) (V i)`, the formal
  character of the direct-sum representation equals the sum of individual
  characters.

    weight-space membership for direct-sum reps.

    `⨁ j, glWeightSpace ρⱼ μ ≃ₗ glWeightSpace (Rep.directSum ρ) μ`.
  * `finrank_glWeightSpace_directSum` — the finrank identity derived from
    the equivalence via `Module.finrank_directSum`.
* The main theorem `formalCharacter_directSum` then follows by coefficient
  extraction via `formalCharacter_coeff`.
