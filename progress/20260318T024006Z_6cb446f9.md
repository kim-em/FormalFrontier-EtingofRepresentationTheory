## Accomplished

Proved `columnFDRep_surjective` (sorry #1 of 2) in `Infrastructure/IrreducibleEnumeration.lean` — the main deliverable of issue #1059.

**What was proved:**
Every simple `FDRep k G` is isomorphic to some column representation `D.columnFDRep i₀` from the Wedderburn-Artin decomposition. The proof:
1. Uses Schur's lemma to show each central idempotent acts as a scalar c_i on a simple W
2. Shows c_i ∈ {0,1} (idempotent) and ∑ c_i = 1, so exactly one c_{i₀} = 1
3. Constructs an orbit basis φ j = asAlgebraHom(iso.symm(Pi.single i₀ (Matrix.single j j₀ 1))) w₀
4. Proves equivariance via a LinearMap L from matrices to W.V
5. Builds an FDRep morphism, proves it nonzero, applies Schur's lemma for the iso

**Helper lemmas added:**
- `Matrix.one_eq_sum_single`: 1 = ∑ j, Matrix.single j j 1
- `Matrix.mul_single_eq_sum`: M * single j j₀ 1 = ∑ a, M a j • single a j₀ 1
