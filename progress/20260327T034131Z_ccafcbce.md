### Key technical discoveries
- `simp [DirectSum.component.of]` and `simp [DirectSum.lof_eq_of, DirectSum.of_apply]` don't fire on local `let lof := DirectSum.lof k _ _` bindings because simp can't see through the local binding
- Working pattern: `DFinsupp.finset_sum_apply` (via `rw`) distributes application over sums, then `simp_rw` with explicit `show` to convert local `lof` to `DFinsupp.single`, then `DFinsupp.single_apply` collapses the sum
- `Submodule.mem_map.mp` (not `mem_map_equiv.mp`) works for destructuring membership proofs
- The `▸` notation needs syntactically matching RHS; use intermediate `have` to force the right form
