**Backward direction proof (contrapositive):**
1. If g₀ is not covered by X, conjugation-invariance of X implies no conjugate x⁻¹g₀x lies in any H ∈ X
2. Therefore all induced characters Ind_H^G(χ_W) vanish at g₀ (each summand in the Frobenius formula is 0)
3. The ℚ-span of these characters also vanishes at g₀ (by span induction)
4. But the trivial character equals 1 at g₀, contradiction
