1. Define X : V×W → V×W by X(v,w) = (Bw, Av)

   - For each cyclic generator g=(v,w) of order k, either (v,0) or (0,w) has order k

     in orthogonal spaces, so at least one component is nonzero
5. Group chains into two nonempty sets → complementary product-compatible decomposition
