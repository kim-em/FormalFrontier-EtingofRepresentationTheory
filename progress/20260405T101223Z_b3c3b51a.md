## Accomplished

Introduced the tabloid representation infrastructure to fix the definitional mismatch in #2140:

1. **`TabloidRepresentation n la`**: Abbreviation for `Finsupp (Tabloid n la) ℂ` — the free ℂ-module with basis indexed by tabloids (the correct home for polytabloids).

2. **`polytabloidTab T`**: Polytabloid e_T in M^λ, defined as Σ_{q ∈ Q_λ} sign(q) · {q⁻¹ · σ_T}. This is the mathematically correct definition.

3. **Key lemmas (all sorry-free)**:
   - `toTabloid_inv_mul_injective`: different column perms give different tabloids
   - `polytabloidTab_coeff_self`: coefficient of {T} in e_T is 1
   - `polytabloidTab_coeff_dominance`: nonzero coefficient implies tabloid dominance
   - `polytabloidTab_syt_dominance`: variant for SYT pairs

4. **`polytabloidTab_linearIndependent`**: Polytabloids are linearly independent in M^λ. Sorry-free proof via dominance triangularity, reusing the existing `exists_dominance_maximal` infrastructure.
