### Documentation
- Documented the 4-step chain for the algebraMap preservation proof:
  1. `eB₁.symm`: `c • LinearMap.id ↦ c • 𝟙 X`
  2. `fRing`: `c • 𝟙 X ↦ c • 𝟙 (F.obj X)` (k-linearity of functor)
  3. `αRing`: `c • 𝟙 (F.obj X) ↦ c • 𝟙 Y` (conjugation preserves scalars)
  4. `eB₂`: `c • 𝟙 Y ↦ c • LinearMap.id`

### Discovered Blocker
A Lean typeclass diamond prevents closing the proof even assuming `equivFunctorLinear`:
- `ModuleCat.instSMulHom` vs `DistribMulAction.toDistribSMul.toSMul` on categorical Hom types
- Traces down to `LieAlgebra.ofAssociativeAlgebra.toModule` vs `RestrictScalars.module` for `Module k B` instances
- Steps h1, h2, h3, h4 individually verified correct, but `rw`/`exact` fails to match across the diamond
