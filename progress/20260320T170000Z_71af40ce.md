## Accomplished

Worked on issue #1517: GL₂ normalizer infrastructure for elliptic subgroup.

1. **Proved `frobeniusMatrix_sq_eq_one`**: The Frobenius matrix σ satisfies σ² = 1 in GL₂(𝔽_q).
   Key technique: use `change` + `simp only [Etingof.GL2.frobeniusMatrix, dif_neg hn]` + `congr; exact Subsingleton.elim _ _` to resolve instance-matching between internal basis of `frobeniusMatrix` and local `let b`. The Frobenius squared identity follows from `GaloisField.card` and `FiniteField.pow_card`.

2. **Proved `frobeniusMatrix_conj`**: The conjugation identity σ⁻¹ · embed(α) · σ = embed(α^q).
   Removed duplicate `haveI : Fintype (GaloisField p n)` (already from `[Fintype ...]` in statement) to avoid Fintype instance conflicts. Proof strategy: unfold both `frobeniusMatrix.val` and `fieldExtEmbed.val` to `toMatrix` and `leftMulMatrix` form, convert to linear map equality via `congr 1; ext x`, then use `map_mul` (σ is a ring hom) and `hσσ` (Frobenius squared is identity).
