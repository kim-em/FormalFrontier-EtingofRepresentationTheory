## Current frontier

The remaining sorry in `formalCharacter_detTwist_eq_shift` requires:
1. Proving weight spaces of det-twisted rep at weights with zero component are trivial
2. Sum reindexing to show formal character = (∏ Xᵢ) · formalCharacter(original)

Both are blocked by a **typeclass diamond** between `FDRep.of` and `SchurModuleSubmodule`:
- `FDRep.of_ρ'` unfolds the representation, but applying `schurModuleRep` to `v : ↑(FDRep.of detTwisted).V` fails instance synthesis
- `change`/`show` with mixed FDRep types triggers "failed to synthesize instance of type class"
- `rw` can't match patterns across the type boundary (different carrier type annotations)
- The diamond is `Field.toSemifield.toCommSemiring` vs `CommRing.toCommSemiring`
