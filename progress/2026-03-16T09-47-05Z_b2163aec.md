- **Theorem 3.10.2** (Tensor product irreducibility): Part (i) uses invariant-submodule characterization — the only k-submodules of V ⊗ W closed under all (a ⊗ b)-actions are {0} and V ⊗ W. Key design decisions:
- Used `Algebra.lsmul k k V : A →ₐ[k] Module.End k V` pattern for the representation map (matching Theorem 3.2.2)

- Krull-Schmidt uses `iSupIndep` from `Mathlib.Order.SupIndep` for internal direct sum independence
