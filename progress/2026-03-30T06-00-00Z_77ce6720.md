Restructured the broken proof of `q2_nontrivial_decomp` in `Problem6_9_1.lean`.

The previous approach projected complementary X-invariant submodules M₁, M₂ of V×W onto V and W, but this is fundamentally incorrect: projections of complementary subspaces are NOT complementary in general. The two `sorry`s at lines 1161/1163 (IsCompl pV qV and IsCompl pW qW) were unprovable with this approach.

- New lemma `off_diagonal_nilpotent_product_decomp` correctly states that for an off-diagonal nilpotent operator X(v,w) = (Bw, Av) with dim(ker A) + dim(ker B) ≥ 2, a product-compatible decomposition exists
- `q2_nontrivial_decomp` now delegates directly to this lemma (single line)
- Removed ~166 lines of broken proof infrastructure
- Reduced sorrys from 2 (unprovable) to 1 (correctly stated)
