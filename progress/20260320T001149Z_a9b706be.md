## Next step

- Prove `hook_quotient_identity_yd`. Known proof strategies:
  - **Greene-Nijenhuis-Wilf hook walk**: Define random walk on cells,
    show each cell contributes exactly 1 to the sum. Requires probability theory.
  - **Algebraic via Frobenius**: Use the determinantal formula dim V_λ = n!/∏h(i,j)
    proved differently (via Frobenius character formula), then derive the identity
    from branching rule + FRT.
  - **Direct induction**: Induct on |μ|, relate F(μ) to F(μ\c₀). Requires tracking
    how hook quotients change under corner removal — infrastructure for this is now
    available (removeCorner_hookLength_row/col/other).
