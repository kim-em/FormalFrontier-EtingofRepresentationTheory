## Accomplished

Proved the character formula (part iv) of Theorem 5.27.1 sorry-free (issue #1782, PR #1872).

The character formula is a Frobenius-type formula for induced representations of semidirect products G ⋉ A:
$$\chi_V(a, g) = \frac{1}{|G_x|} \sum_{h \in G: hgh^{-1} \in G_x} x(h(a)) \chi_U(hgh^{-1})$$

Key proof elements:
- **Sum reindexing** via h ↦ h⁻¹ using `Equiv.inv G`
- **Right-H-invariance** of the summand, proved using `stab_char_inv` (stabilizer character invariance) and `FDRep.char_conj` (character is a class function)
- **Fiber decomposition** via `sum_right_invariant_eq` helper lemma, decomposing sum over G into |H| × sum over G/H
- **Fintype instance diamond** resolved by removing explicit `haveI` and letting `classical` provide instances uniformly
