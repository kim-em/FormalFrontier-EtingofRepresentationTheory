## Accomplished

Completed issue #989: Proved linear independence of standard monomials in the Weyl algebra (Proposition 2.7.1).

The proof constructs a polynomial representation ρ: WeylAlgebra k → End_k(k[X]) sending x to multiplication by X and y to d/dX. The Leibniz rule ensures ρ respects the Weyl algebra relation yx = xy + 1. The key computation shows ρ(x^i y^j)(X^j) = j! X^i, which enables coefficient extraction via strong induction on j to prove each coefficient g(i,j) = 0.

Requires `CharZero k` and `NoZeroDivisors k` hypotheses (needed to cancel j! in the induction step).
