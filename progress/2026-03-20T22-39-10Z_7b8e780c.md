- **Proved `equivalence_preserves_simple`**: An equivalence of preadditive categories
  sends simple objects to simple objects. The proof transports a mono via the inverse
  equivalence, uses simplicity in the source category, then reflects the iso/zero
  property back through the equivalence.
- **Proved `moritaEquivalent_preserves_simple`**: Specialization to module categories.

  `∃ (e : A) (he : IsIdempotentElem e), Nonempty (CornerRing e ≃ₐ[k] B)`.
  Uses the `CornerRing` infrastructure from #1509.
