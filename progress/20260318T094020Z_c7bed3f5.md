Fixed incorrect `d_eq_finrank` theorem statement and made `IrreducibleEnumeration.lean` sorry-free.

**Core fix**: The old `d_eq_finrank` claimed `D.d i = finrank k (V i)` for any complete enumeration V of non-isomorphic simples. This is false — V could order simples differently than `D.columnFDRep`, swapping blocks of different sizes.
