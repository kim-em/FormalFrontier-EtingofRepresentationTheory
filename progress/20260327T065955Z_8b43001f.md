### Identified fundamental LEFT/RIGHT coset mismatch

The TabloidSetoid uses LEFT cosets (σ₁ * σ₂⁻¹ ∈ P_λ), so LEFT multiplication by P_λ preserves tabloids. But the polytabloid expansion e_T = Σ sign(q) · of(σ_T * p * q) has P_λ acting in the MIDDLE (not on the left), so σ_T * p * q ≠ p' * σ_T * q for p' ∈ P_λ in general (since σ_T doesn't normalize P_λ).

This means:
- The expansion does NOT simplify to Σ_q sign(q) · toTabloid(σ_T * q)
- The LEFT-action `column_perm_dominance` (q⁻¹ * σ_T) cannot be applied
- A new proof of `pq_dominance` is needed, or the convention must change

## Current frontier

The key mathematical blocker is `pq_dominance`: proving tabloidDominates la σ_T (σ_T * p * q) for all p ∈ P_λ, q ∈ Q_λ.

The inequality reduces to: ∀ k, i, |{e ≤ k : row_T(p(q(e))) < i}| ≤ |{e ≤ k : row_T(e) < i}| where row_T(e) = row of entry e in T.

This is NOT a simple per-column inequality (per-canonical-column decomposition can fail for individual columns). The existing LEFT-action proof technique does not adapt.

## Next step

Three possible approaches to resolve the blocker:

1. **Prove `pq_dominance` directly**: Need a new proof technique. The inequality |q(L_k) ∩ S| ≤ |L_k ∩ S| where L_k = {0,...,k}, S = {e : row_T(p(e)) < i}, q ∈ Q_λ requires a global argument (per-column decomposition fails). May need RSK or rearrangement theory.

2. **Switch TabloidSetoid to RIGHT cosets**: Replace `σ₁ * σ₂⁻¹ ∈ P_λ` with `σ₁⁻¹ * σ₂ ∈ P_λ`. Then RIGHT mult by P_λ preserves tabloids, and the expansion simplifies to Σ_q sign(q) · toTabloid_R(σ_T * q). The existing `column_perm_dominance` (LEFT version) would then apply. Requires refactoring ~200 lines in TabloidModule.lean.

3. **Alternative independence proof**: Use representation theory (dim V_λ = |SYT(λ)| + spanning → independence), or a bilinear form on the tabloid module, bypassing dominance entirely.

Option 2 (RIGHT coset switch) is probably the cleanest path.

## Blockers

- `pq_dominance` is a genuine mathematical gap in the current proof strategy
- The LEFT coset convention makes the tabloid projection approach fundamentally harder
