## Accomplished

Completed issue #1384: Prove hook walk row completeness (∑_c w(u,c) = 1).

1. **Proved `hookCellsExcl_disjoint`**: The arm cells (same row, to the right) and leg cells (same column, below) are disjoint finsets.

2. **Proved `hookCellsExcl_card`**: The cardinality of `hookCellsExcl i j` equals `hookLength i j - 1`. Uses disjointness of arm/leg, injectivity of the image maps, and `Nat.card_Ico`.

3. **Proved `hookCellsExcl_subset_cells`**: Every cell in the hook (excluding the center) is a member of `μ.cells`.

4. **Proved `hookWalkWeight_sum_corners`** (main deliverable): For any cell `(i,j) ∈ μ.cells`, the sum of hook walk weights over all outer corners equals 1. Proof by well-founded induction on `hookLength`:
   - Base case (h=1): Cell is an outer corner, sum collapses to single term via `hookWalkWeight_corner`/`hookWalkWeight_ne_corner`.
   - Inductive case (h≥2): Unfold recursive definition, factor out `1/(h-1)`, swap double sum via `Finset.sum_comm`, apply IH to each inner sum (gives ∑_v 1 = h-1), then `(h-1)/(h-1) = 1`.
