## Accomplished

Completed issue #1499: Define principal series representations for GL₂(𝔽_q) (Theorem 5.25.2 definitions).

1. **Defined `Etingof.GL2.principalSeries`**: The induced representation Ind_B^G ℂ_{χ₁,χ₂}, realized as a covariance submodule of (G → ℂ) with right-translation action. Functions f satisfy f(bg) = χ₁(b₀₀)·χ₂(b₁₁)·f(g) for b in the Borel subgroup.

2. **Defined `Etingof.GL2.detChar`**: The 1-dimensional representation ℂ_μ where g acts by scalar multiplication by μ(det g).

3. **Defined `Etingof.GL2.complementW`**: The irreducible complement W_μ, defined as the intersection of the covariance submodule V(μ,μ) with the kernel of the augmentation functional ∑_g f(g)·μ(det g)⁻¹.
