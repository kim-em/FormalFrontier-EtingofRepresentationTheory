## Accomplished

Claimed #2331 ("Prove leaf-case sorries in `non_adjacent_branches_infinite_type`"),
then **decomposed** it into three sub-issues after Step 4b scope assessment
indicated the work far exceeded a single session. No code changes in this turn
— the deliverable is the decomposition itself.

**Why decomposition.** The `leaf_case` helper (line ~4953 of
`InfiniteTypeConstructions.lean`) requires embedding one of three extended-Dynkin
forbidden subgraphs (D̃_n caterpillar, Ẽ₇ = T(1,3,3), or T(1,2,5) = Ẽ₈) into
the ambient acyclic quiver under a 4-parameter case analysis:

1. Neighbor-of-v₀ (non-chain side) degree: 1 vs ≥ 2
2. w's two other neighbors' degrees
3. Path length between v₀ and w

Existing infrastructure covers the `d5tildeAdj` case (adjacent branches,
~500 LoC) and the `etilde7Adj`/`t125Adj` bases (standalone infinite families),
but no arbitrary-length D̃_n adjacency exists. Building this mirrors the
D̃_5 pattern and is itself ~800-1200 LoC of new code. All three warning
signs from the `agent-worker-flow` Step 4b applied.

**Sub-issues created** (via `coordination plan --label feature`):

- **#2347 — Build D̃_n infrastructure for arbitrary path-length (n ≥ 6).**
  New `dtildeAdj (n : ℕ) (h : 3 ≤ n)` with non-finite-type proof. Mirrors
  `d5tildeAdj`'s proof of indecomposability via explicit family of reps
  with distinct dim vectors. ~800-1200 LoC.
- **#2348 — Prove leaf_case caterpillar sub-case (D̃_n embedding).** Blocked
  on #2347 (`coordination add-dep 2348 2347`). Handles the sub-case where
  the neighbor-of-v₀ has degree 1 and w's two other neighbors have degree 1,
  path length n ≥ 5. ~150-250 LoC.
- **#2349 — Prove leaf_case non-caterpillar sub-cases (Ẽ₇/T(1,2,5)).** The
  "branches thicken" sub-cases, embedding one of the existing Ẽ₇ or T(1,2,5)
  adjacency matrices. ~500-800 LoC.

Then skipped #2331 with `coordination skip 2331 "Decomposed into #2347, #2348, #2349"`.
