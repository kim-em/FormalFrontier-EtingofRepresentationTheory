### Scope assessment

`garnir_term_col_standard_classified` is the Difficulty-8
combinatorial heart of the Specht module straightening theorem
(James Ch. 7-8 / Fulton Ch. 7). Its conclusion is:

```
∃ q₀ ∈ ColumnSubgroup, q₀·w·q⁻¹·σ is col-std AND
  (tabloidStrictDominates la σ (q₀·w·q⁻¹·σ) ∨
    (toTabloid = toTabloid σ ∧ rowInvCount' < rowInvCount' σ))
```

Structurally this splits cleanly into:

1. An **always-true** `tabloidDominates la σ (q₀·w·q⁻¹·σ)` (which
   upgrades to `tabloidStrictDominates` whenever tabloids differ).
2. A **same-tabloid** witness that `rowInvCount' (q₀·w·q⁻¹·σ) <
   rowInvCount' σ`.

Given both, the residual assembly of #2451 is a ~10-line `by_cases`
on tabloid equality. Each half is a substantial combinatorial
theorem in its own right (50-200+ Lean lines; the always-true
dominance half generalizes the SYT-specific `column_perm_dominance`
to column-standard σ and adds cross-column Garnir pigeonhole).
