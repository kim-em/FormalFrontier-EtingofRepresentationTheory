## Technical notes

The `conv_lhs` pattern caused both sums to rewrite unintentionally — use
`nth_rewrite 1 [h_reindex]` instead for "rewrite the first occurrence only".

For `w * swap(a, b) * w⁻¹ = swap(w a, w b)`, prefer `refine Equiv.ext fun x`
over `ext x` to avoid `Fin.ext` being applied automatically (which
introduces `↑`/`Fin.val` coercions unexpectedly).

`Equiv.swap_apply_of_ne_of_ne` needs explicit `(a := ...) (b := ...)`
arguments under `classical` to avoid `DecidableEq ?m` typeclass stuck
errors.
