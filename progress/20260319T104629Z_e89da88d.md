## Current frontier

The three sorry'd theorems require deep combinatorial arguments:
- **Linear independence**: needs a partial order on tabloids (dominance) and a triangularity argument showing each polytabloid has a unique leading tabloid
- **Spanning**: needs the straightening algorithm (rewriting non-standard polytabloids via Garnir relations as sums of standard ones)
- These are the core technical challenges; once both are done, the dimension theorem follows
