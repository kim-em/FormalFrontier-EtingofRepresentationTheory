### Why the sketch fails (same structural issue as #2454)

The sketch's "per-column count ≤" argument fails because
min(i, a) + min(i, b) is **maximized by balance**, not preserved.
σ has extreme column imbalance (col 0 entirely large, col 2 entirely
small). The Garnir sandwich can balance this pair, which INCREASES
ρ's cumulative count at thresholds in the small range — exactly
violating weak dominance.
