## Accomplished

### Issue #1355: Proved `dynkin_edge_count`
Proved that a Dynkin diagram on n vertices has exactly n-1 edges (tree characterization). This completes the second deliverable of #1355 (first was `dynkin_no_cycle` in PR #1372).

**Proof strategy:**
- **Upper bound** (edgeCount ≤ n-1): Positive definiteness of the Cartan form applied to the all-ones vector gives B(1,...,1) = 2n - 2·edgeCount > 0, hence edgeCount < n.
- **Lower bound** (n-1 ≤ edgeCount): Constructed a `SimpleGraph` from the adjacency matrix, proved it's connected by converting list-based path connectivity to `SimpleGraph.Reachable`, and used Mathlib's `Connected.card_vert_le_card_edgeSet_add_one`.

**Helper lemmas added:**
- `adj_sum_eq_degree`: row sum of a 0-1 adjacency matrix equals vertex degree
- `list_path_reachable`: converts `IsDynkinDiagram`'s list-based path connectivity to `SimpleGraph.Reachable`
