## Accomplished

### Fixed polytabloid_syt_dominance direction (issue #1928)

Discovered and corrected a direction bug in `polytabloid_syt_dominance` in TabloidModule.lean. The original conclusion claimed `tabloidDominates la (sytPerm n la T₂) (sytPerm n la T₁)` (T₂ dominates T₁), but the correct direction is `tabloidDominates la (sytPerm n la T₁) (sytPerm n la T₂)` (T₁ dominates T₂).

**Evidence**: Concrete counterexample with partition (2,1,1). T₁=[0 2|1|3], T₂=[0 3|1|2]: e_{T₁}(σ_{T₂})≠0 but tabloidCumulCount(σ_{T₂}, k=2, i=1)=1 < 2=tabloidCumulCount(σ_{T₁}, k=2, i=1), so T₂ does NOT dominate T₁.
