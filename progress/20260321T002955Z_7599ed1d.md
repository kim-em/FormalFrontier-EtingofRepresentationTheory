## Accomplished

- Identified that `Etingof.MoritaStructural` (Chapter9 and Infrastructure versions) has a **false statement**: it claims `B ≅ eAe` for ANY Morita equivalent A, B without size constraints. Counterexample: k and M₂(k) are Morita equivalent but M₂(k) cannot be eke for any e ∈ k.
- Fixed the statement by adding `IsBasicAlgebra k B` and `Module.Finite` hypotheses, making it mathematically correct (basic algebras always embed as corner rings of any Morita-equivalent algebra).
- Updated all downstream uses in `Corollary9_7_3.lean` to pass the new hypotheses.
- Verified full Chapter 9 builds successfully.
- Created PR #1559 with the fix.
