### Proof strategy documented:
The proof decomposes into:
1. **Decompose**: For basic Bᵢ over alg closed k, use `Theorem_9_2_1_i` + `isInternal_leftIdeals` to decompose Bᵢ ≅ ⊕ Pᵢ(j) into indecomposable projectives
2. **Preserve**: F sends each P₁(j) to an indecomposable projective B₂-module; F bijects simples
3. **Match**: By `indecomposable_projective_iso_of_hom`, F(P₁(j)) ≅ P₂(σ(j)) for a bijection σ
4. **Assemble**: ⊕ F(P₁(j)) ≅ ⊕ P₂(σ(j)) ≅ ⊕ P₂(j) ≅ B₂
