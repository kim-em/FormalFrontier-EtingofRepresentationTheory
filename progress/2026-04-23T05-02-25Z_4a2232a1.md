## Accomplished

Fixed the circularity in `garnir_twisted_in_lower_span` (issue #2413):

1. Added new helper lemma `twistedPolytabloid_row_eq`: for w ∈ P_λ (row-preserving),
   `twistedPolytabloid w σ = ψ_σ` (tabloids are equivalence classes up to row
   permutation).

2. Added hypothesis `hw_row : w ∉ RowSubgroup n la` to `garnir_twisted_in_lower_span`.
   This excludes the problematic row-preserving permutations (e.g. swap(p₁, p₂))
   which make the original theorem statement false. The core combinatorial sorry
   remains — the new hypothesis just makes it provable in principle.

3. Restructured `garnir_straightening_step` with a three-way split of the T-sum:
   - **Q part** (w ∈ Q_λ): each term equals ψ (sign² = 1), sum = k·ψ.
   - **P\\Q part** (w ∈ P_λ \\ Q_λ): using a structural characterization — the
     only non-identity row-preserving G-supported permutation is swap(p₁, p₂) —
     this filter is a singleton, and the sum is -ψ.
   - **Neither part** (w ∉ P_λ ∪ Q_λ): uses the (fixed) `garnir_twisted_in_lower_span`,
     and lies in the lower span L.

   Combined: k·ψ = -(neither sum) ∈ L. If k ≥ 1, divide by k to conclude ψ ∈ L.
