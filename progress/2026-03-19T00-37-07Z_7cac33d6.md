- Key proof steps:

  2. Any A-linear endomorphism φ lies in centralizer(A) (by A-linearity)
  3. f ∈ centralizer(centralizer(A)) commutes with all such φ, hence is (End_A E)-linear
  4. Lift f to `End (End A E) E` and apply `jacobson_density` with a finite k-spanning set
  5. Extend agreement from spanning set to all of E by k-linearity
