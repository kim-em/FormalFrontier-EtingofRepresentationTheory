## Accomplished

Analyzed issue #1928 (prove polytabloid_linearIndependent via tabloid projection) and determined that the core sorry `polytabloid_syt_dominance` requires a fundamentally different approach than what the issue proposed.

Key findings:
1. **LEFT vs RIGHT column dominance mismatch**: The existing `column_perm_dominance` (proved) handles LEFT multiplication by q⁻¹, but the PQ decomposition σ_{T₂} = σ_{T₁} · p · q involves RIGHT multiplication by q. These are fundamentally different operations on tabloids.
2. **Row permutation doesn't simplify**: While `toTabloid(σ·p) = toTabloid(σ)` for p ∈ P_λ, this only eliminates the p factor, leaving the RIGHT q-multiplication problem.
3. **Issue's proposed approach insufficient**: The tabloid coefficient extraction approach reduces to the same RIGHT-mult dominance problem.
