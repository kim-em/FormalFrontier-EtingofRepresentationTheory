## Accomplished

Proved `Dn_count` — the last remaining `sorry` in `Example6_4_9.lean`. This lemma states that the number of positive roots of the D_n Dynkin diagram equals n*(n-1).

**Proof strategy** (induction on n):
- **Base cases**: D₄ = 12 and D₅ = 20 by `native_decide`
- **Inductive step**: Split `rootCountFinset(D_{m+1})` by first coordinate v₀ ∈ {0, 1, 2}:
  - v₀ = 0: bijection with D_m roots via tail map (`Dn_filter_zero_card`)
  - v₀ = 2: impossible (`Dn_no_coord2_at_zero`, proved by SOS/PD induction)
  - v₀ = 1, v₁ = 0: exactly 1 root (the unit vector e₀)
  - v₀ = 1, v₁ = 1: bijection with D_m roots having first coord = 1
  - v₀ = 1, v₁ = 2: bijection with `qFourFinset(D_m)` which has card 1

**Helper lemmas added**: `D5_count`, `Dn_filter_zero_card`, `Dn_no_coord2_at_zero`, `qFourFinset`, `D4_qfour`, `qFourFinset_peel`, `qFourFinset_card`.
