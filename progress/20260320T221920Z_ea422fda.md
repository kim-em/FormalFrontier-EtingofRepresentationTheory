2. **`hRHS` rhoShift sum rewriting (was line 1548)**: Proved F.sum(rhoShift) = ∑ i ∈ range(min k n), (n-1-i) via `Finset.sum_nbij` bijecting F with range(min k n).

4. **k ≥ n case (was line 1559)**: When k > n, both sides equal ∑ j : Fin n, j.val. Used Fin.rev bijection to show ∑(n-1-i) = ∑ i.
