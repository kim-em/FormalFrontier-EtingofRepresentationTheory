## Accomplished

Proved `Example5_19_3_symmetric`: the Sₙ-invariant subspace of V⊗ⁿ is
isomorphic to the n-th symmetric power Sym[k]^n V.

The proof uses the canonical projection `SymmetricPower.mk` as the forward
map. Injectivity follows from a Reynolds operator argument: the symmetrization
sum `Σ_σ σ·x` factors through `SymmetricPower.mk` (via `AddCon.addConGen_le`),
and on invariant elements equals `n! · x`, so if `mk(a) = mk(b)` then
`n! · (a - b) = 0`, giving `a = b` by `CharZero`. Surjectivity uses the
preimage `(n!)⁻¹ · symSum(x)` for any lift `x` of `y ∈ Sym^n V`.

(Submodule of Clifford algebra) and `PiTensorProduct`. Per project guidelines,

## Blockers

Exterior power proof blocked by Lean coercion issues between `exteriorPower`
(a `Submodule` of `ExteriorAlgebra`, itself built on `CliffordAlgebra`) and
`PiTensorProduct`. Key difficulties:
1. `exteriorPower.linearMap_ext` creates `compAlternatingMap` goals with
   `↑` coercions that `simp` cannot resolve
2. `Fintype.sum_equiv` type mismatches when goals are wrapped in
   `compMultilinearMap`
3. `congr 1` strips one layer of coercion but leaves incompatible goal forms
