## Accomplished

Partial completion of issue #678 (FDRep irreducible enumeration):

- **Proved simplicity** of `columnFDRep i`: each column vector representation is a Simple object in FDRep k G. Chain: `Matrix.instIsSimpleModule` → `IsSimpleModule.compHom` (via surjective projRingHom) → `IsSimpleModule k[G] (columnRep i).asModule` → `Simple (ModuleCat.of k[G] ...)` → `Simple (Rep k G ...)` → `Simple (FDRep k G ...)` via full+faithful functor reflection.

- **Proved injectivity** of `columnFDRep`: if `columnFDRep i ≅ columnFDRep j` then `i = j`. Uses central idempotent argument: `e_i = iso.symm (Pi.single i 1)` acts as identity on V_i and zero on V_j. Extended G-equivariance to k[G]-equivariance via `MonoidAlgebra.induction_on`, then showed any equivariant map V_i → V_j is zero when i ≠ j.

  - `FDRep.simple_of_isSimpleModule_asModule`: bridge from algebraic to categorical simplicity
  - `equivariant_ext`: extends G-equivariance to k[G]-equivariance
  - `IrrepDecomp.isoToLinearEquiv_equivariant`: extracts equivariance from FDRep iso

Reduced sorries from 4 to 2.
