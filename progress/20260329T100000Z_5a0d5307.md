## Accomplished

Deep mathematical analysis of three related issues in the polytabloid/Young symmetrizer chain:

### Issue #1946 (skipped earlier): `column_standard_coset_has_syt'` is FALSE
- Theorem needs σ ∈ sytPerm(T) * P_λ (RIGHT coset)
- Row-sorting gives σ ∈ P_λ * sytPerm(T) (LEFT coset)
- Counterexample: partition (3,1), σ = (13)

### Issue #1928 (skipped this session): tabloid projection approach has same gap
- Claims `column_perm_dominance` (LEFT-mult) gives off-diagonal dominance
- But after PQ decomposition, need tabloidDominates(σ_T', σ_T' * r * q) — RIGHT-mult dominance
- Same fundamental gap as #1942 (RIGHT-mult column dominance)
- Also verified: for partition (3,1), the 3×3 SYT evaluation matrix is singular (e_{T₂}(σ_{T₃}) = e_{T₃}(σ_{T₂}) = 1), confirming dominance argument is essential

### Issue #1969 (skipped this session): LEFT coset fix is insufficient
- Changing to LEFT coset σ = p * sytPerm(T) gives of(σ)*YS = of(p)*polytabloid(T)
- But of(p)*polytabloid(T) ≠ polytabloid(T): the p sits LEFT of sytPerm(T), far from a_λ
- The RIGHT coset works because p⁻¹ ends up adjacent to a_λ inside YS

### Key discovery: structural issue in TabloidSetoid convention
- TabloidSetoid uses LEFT P_λ-cosets (σ₁ * σ₂⁻¹ ∈ P_λ)
- Row-sorting preserves LEFT cosets (same tabloid)
- But absorption needs RIGHT cosets (σ ∈ sytPerm(T) * P_λ)
- These differ because P_λ is not normal in S_n

### Possible fixes identified:
1. **Change TabloidSetoid to RIGHT cosets** (~800 lines rewrite)
2. **Extend induction measure** to (column inversions, row inversions) with row straightening lemma
3. **Prove RIGHT-mult column dominance** independently (issue #1942) to fix polytabloid_syt_dominance
