(1) $V_1 + V_2 + V_3 = V$.

(2) $V_1 \cap V_2 = 0$, $\quad V_1 \cap V_3 = 0$, $\quad V_2 \cap V_3 = 0$.

(3) $V_1 \subseteq V_2 \oplus V_3$, $\quad V_2 \subseteq V_1 \oplus V_3$, $\quad V_3 \subseteq V_1 \oplus V_2$.

But this implies that

$$
