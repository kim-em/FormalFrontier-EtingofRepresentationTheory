Now let $\mathcal{C}$ be a finite abelian category with a projective generator $P$. Let $B = \operatorname{End}(P)^{\mathrm{op}}$ (a finite dimensional algebra acting on the right on $P$). Let $B\text{-fmod}$ denote the category of finite dimensional left $B$-modules. Consider the functor $F : \mathcal{C} \to B\text{-fmod}$ given by the formula $F(M) = \operatorname{Hom}(P, M)$.
