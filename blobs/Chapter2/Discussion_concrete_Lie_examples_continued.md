(4) The algebra $\operatorname{aff}(1)$ of matrices $\begin{pmatrix} * & * \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$.
Its basis consists of $X = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$ and $Y = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$, with $[X, Y] = Y$.
