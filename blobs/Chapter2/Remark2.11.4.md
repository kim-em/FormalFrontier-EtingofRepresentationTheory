
**Remark 2.11.4.** Note that a similar definition to the above can be used to define the tensor product $V \otimes_A W$, where $A$ is any ring, $V$ is a right $A$-module, and $W$ is a left $A$-module. Namely, $V \otimes_A W$ is the abelian group which is the quotient of the group $V \bullet W$ freely generated by formal symbols $v \otimes w$, $v \in V$, $w \in W$, modulo the
relations

$$
(v_1 + v_2) \otimes w - v_1 \otimes w - v_2 \otimes w,
$$
$$
v \otimes (w_1 + w_2) - v \otimes w_1 - v \otimes w_2,
$$
$$
va \otimes w - v \otimes aw, \quad a \in A.
$$
