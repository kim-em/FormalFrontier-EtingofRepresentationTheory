**Proposition 5.14.1.** *We have $\operatorname{Hom}(U_\lambda, V_\mu) = 0$ for $\mu < \lambda$ and $\dim \operatorname{Hom}(U_\lambda, V_\lambda) = 1$. Thus, $U_\lambda = \bigoplus_{\mu \geq \lambda} K_{\mu\lambda} V_\mu$, where $K_{\mu\lambda}$ are nonnegative integers and $K_{\lambda\lambda} = 1$.*
